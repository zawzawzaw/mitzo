Food philosophy

Mitzo reinvents the Chinese dining experience and brings with it a fresh approach and energy. A key feature of Mitzo cuisine is staying true to the philosophy of Cantonese cuisine; focusing on the quality of ingredients and its execution, while retaining its familiarity and authenticity. Through the innovative use of ingredients, each visit brings with it new flavours and a different type of experience. Most dishes are designed for sharing, so guests can try a variety of offerings.
